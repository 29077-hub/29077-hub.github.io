The Problem with AI

Many AI systems operate without transparency. People often do not know when they are interacting with AI or how decisions are being made.

AI can spread misinformation, reinforce bias in hiring or policing, and create deepfake content that misleads the public.

Without regulation, these risks will continue to grow and affect society in harmful ways.
