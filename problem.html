The Problem with AI

Lack of Transparency

Many AI systems operate without users knowing. People often interact with AI without realizing it, making it difficult to trust the information they receive.

Misinformation & Deepfakes

AI can generate fake images, videos, and text that look real. This can spread misinformation and influence public opinion in harmful ways.

Bias and Unfair Decisions

AI systems used in hiring or policing can reflect bias in their data, leading to unfair treatment of certain groups.

Without regulation, these problems will continue to grow and impact society.
