AI Transparency & Accountability Act

Ensuring AI is safe, fair, and transparent

Welcome

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly shaping our world. However, without proper regulation, it can lead to misinformation, bias, and lack of accountability.

The AI Transparency & Accountability Act ensures that AI systems are transparent, tested for safety, and held accountable for harm.

Use the navigation above to learn more about the problem, the rules of the law, and why you should support it.
